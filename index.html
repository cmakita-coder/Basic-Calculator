COMPANY PROFILE

September 2024 Edition

BACKGROUND

Ultimate Emporium (K) Limited (also abbreviated as UEK Ltd) was established to bridge the gap in the market of quality service provision in various professional fields inclusive of Consultancy, Civil Works, and General Supplies etc.

Ultimate Emporium (K) Ltd was formed in 2020 and later incorporated in the year 2024 under the Companies Act.

VISION

Be a leading quality service provider in various professional fields including Consultancy, Civil Works and trading in other merchandise.

MISSION

Provision of services with integrity and efficiency to end user by advocating courtesy, commitment and care with respect to clients for enhancement of nation building.

OUR COMMITMENT

Committed to providing best services and products with courtesy, care and respect to our clients.

THE COMPANY STRUCTURE

The personnel behind this entity are classified in three broad categories:

  1. The Director who is always available.
  2. The Operation Director who is full time and answerable to the Board of Directors.
  3. Other Company employees who are headed by their respective department heads answerable to the Operations Director.
